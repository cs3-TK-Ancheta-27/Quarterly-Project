Yelan Impact

Yelan is a mysterious and cunning woman who operates in the shadows of Liyue. Known as the "Phantom Thief," she excels in acquiring confidential information and completing difficult tasks. Yelan is a valuable asset to the Ministry of Civil Affairs, but her true motives remain shrouded in secrecy.

Despite her enigmatic nature, Yelan is fiercely loyal to those she cares about. She is also a skilled fighter, capable of wielding a bow with unmatched precision. When not carrying out missions, Yelan can often be found gambling or enjoying the finer things in life.

A master of deception and intrigue, Yelan is a force to be reckoned with. Her presence in Liyue is sure to have a profound impact on the events that unfold.
